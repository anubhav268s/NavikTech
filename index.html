Drive Safer with Real-Time Intelligence

Foresight continuously monitors your driving environment without compromising your privacy. Powered by a proprietary Weighted Multi-Factor Risk Model, it evaluates Speed, Weather, Traffic, Light Conditions, and Road Types to calculate your safety score every second.

Operating efficiently in the background with a 9.5/10 Security Audit Score, it provides zero-latency alerts without ever sending your location to a tracking server. Your safety and your data are entirely in your hands.

The Foresight Risk Engine evaluates your performance and generates a detailed report outlining your safety proficiency:

Safety Score Grading Scale

A+ (Excellent): ≥ 90  |  A (Good): 80-89.9
B (Average): 70-79.9  |  C (Needs Improvement): 60-69.9
D (High Risk): 50-59.9  |  F (Highest Risk): < 50

Inside the Risk Engine

Your grade isn't just based on average speed; it's calculated moment-to-moment. Speed accounts for 40% of your total risk factor.

A or A+ (Elite): Maintain smooth, moderate speeds (under 65 km/h).
B or C (Average): Speeds over 90 km/h, or driving in poor weather/traffic.
Erratic Driving: Zig-zagging triggers an instant -10 point penalty!
